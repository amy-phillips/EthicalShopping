Ethical Shopping Helper is a Chrome Extension that highlights products on the Sainburys/Tesco/Ocado website based on their ratings from Ethical Consumer.

You can try it out using the freely available ratings tables on Ethical Consumer for products such as Cereal, Honey, Butter and Spreads.

To unlock all the ratings you will need an Ethical Consumer subscription - you can or at https://www.ethicalconsumer.org
